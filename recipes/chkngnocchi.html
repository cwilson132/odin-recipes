Chicken and Gnocchi Soup

Description

An easy to make, delicious, high protein chicken and dumpling soup made with gnocchi.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chickne breast
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 packet of powdered chicken gravy mix
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 package of gnocchi

Instructions

  1. Trim excess fat from chicken breast and dice if you like. I prefer to leave it whole for now. Then, place in a crockpot.
  2. In a large bowl, add your chicken broth, gravy packet and cream of chicken soup, and mix until the gravy powder has combined well with the liquid ingredients.
  3. Once you have everything mixed together, add it to the crockpot with the chicken breast and cook on high 4-6 hours.
  4. In the meantime, you'll want to prepare the gnocchi as per the instrutions on the package and set aside.
  5. After the chicken has finished cooking, separate it from the liquid in the crockpot and dice or shred if you haven't already.
  6. Portion out your gnocchi and chicken depending on how many servings you want.
  7. Add as much of the left over liquid from the crockpot as you desire to each container.
  8. Enjoy your delicious meal prep chicken and gnocchi soup.
