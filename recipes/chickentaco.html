Chicken Tacos

Description

Chicken tacos made with a cheesy taco seaoning and greek yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of bonless, skinless chicken breast
  • 3 packets of Mccormick cheesy taco seasoning
  • 600 grams of nonfat greek yogurt
  • 1 can of rotel tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Dice chicken breast, and cook thoroughly
  2. While chicken is cooking, add in your seasoning and rotel
  3. Here I like to shred my chicken, but you can leave it diced if you like
  4. Once chicken is properly cooked and most of the moisture has evaporated, transfer to a large bowl
  5. Mix in your 600 grams of nonfat greek yogurt
  6. Now enjoy with your choice of rice or tortillas
