Old Fashioned

Description

The old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and water, adding whiskey, and garnishing with orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry. It is traditionally served in an old fashioned glass, which predated the cocktail.

Ingredients

  • Bourbon of your choice
  • 1 orange for the peel
  • Angostura bitters
  • A sugar cube or simple syrup
  • A single large piece of ice

Instructions

  1. In your whiskey glass, add your sugar cube and 3-6 dashes of the angostura bitters.
  2. Muddle until the sugar and bitters until the cube has been broken down.
  3. Add your ice and 2-3 ounces of bourbon.
  4. Stir for 25-30 seconds or until your desired dilution is reached.
  5. Using a peeler, peel off a piece of the orange peel that is around 3 inches long.
  6. Squeeze the peel over the glass to express the oils.
  7. wipe the peel along the rim of the glass then garnish the drink with the peel.
  8. All that's left to do now is sit back and enoy your Old Fashioned.
